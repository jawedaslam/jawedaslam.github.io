about me

Jawed Aslam

Jawed Aslam is an emerging artist, musicianand cinematographer from Kerala living in New Delhi. Jawed has completed his Masters in Painting from Jamia Millia IslamiaUniversity. He has worked as a commercial artist and has exhibited both group and solo show across India. His works are also part of several art collections in Qatar, Europe and, Dubai. Jawed enjoys making artworks in different expressions that reflects the mind and soul of him. He can broadly be considered as an expressionist artist. As also being a musician he draws his inspiration from within, it originates from an internal urge to connect to his subconscious. His deep visions and passionate engagement with music and colours has played an important role in shaping his works. Describing his style of work Jawed avers, “Music has remained constant as part of my oeuvre over the years in my career as a painter”. Steeped in dreams, places and memories, his works defines easy identification with representational figures. Jawed’s work echoes life and emotions in all its layers moving along with the music. His images can be read like journals which includes his feelings, thoughts, the reinterpretation of old art works, and daily happenings. His works are mostly oil or acrylic on canvas. The beauty in his art has an influence from the later works by Francisco Goya (Spanish artist), like-using of pastel and dull colours with facial forms and expressions. He picks up a theme and makes a series of paintings on them. When the idea gets repetitive, he waits for another inspiration to strike and move on to the next series. Jawed has lots of potential which hopefully will result into the years to come.
